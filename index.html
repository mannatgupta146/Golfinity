ABOUT US

Welcome to Golfinity, India’s premier golf destination featuring a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range powered by Toptracer Range technology. Perfect for all skill levels, our facility includes a practice green, bunker, cozy coffee shop, and an in-house American Golf Store. For family fun, we also offer two exciting 18-hole outdoor dinosaur-themed crazy golf courses — making Golfinity a complete golfing experience for everyone.

TOPRACER RANGE

Toptracer Range brings the thrill of advanced golf technology to every swing. With real-time shot tracking, detailed feedback, and fun game modes, it transforms traditional driving ranges into interactive, data-driven experiences, perfect for both practice and play.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Adventure Golf at Golfinity offers a fun-filled experience for all ages with exciting, dinosaur-themed 18-hole courses. Designed to entertain families, friends, and groups, each hole blends challenge and charm, making it a perfect outing for casual fun, celebrations, or a unique day out.

GOLF LESSONS

Golf Lessons at Golfinity provide expert training for golfers of all levels. With personalized guidance from experienced instructors, you learn proper techniques, improve your swing, and build confidence in a supportive and professional environment.
