ABOUT US

Welcome to Golfinity, India’s premier golf destination featuring a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range powered by Toptracer Range technology. Perfect for all skill levels, our facility includes a practice green, bunker, cozy coffee shop, and an in-house American Golf Store. For family fun, we also offer two exciting 18-hole outdoor dinosaur-themed crazy golf courses — making Golfinity a complete golfing experience for everyone.
